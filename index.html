Who Am I?

My name is Minh To and I am currently a senior studying Software Engineering at the University of California, Irvine.

The major drives that have been pushing me for the past years to follow this path stem from my early interaction with technologies as well as my amazement at the time. At a very young age, I found myself fascinated by the "superpowers" that my dad had using photo editing softwares in his work. Whenever I saw him using Adobe Photoshop to "magically" beautify or transform his photos, I would ask him out of excitement tomorrow do it again, but this time on my photos so I can brag about how photogenic I am to my friends. From that moment on, it is my desire to develop softwares that will have significant impacts on others' lives.
